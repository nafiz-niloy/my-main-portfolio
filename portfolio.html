Surrogate Models for Neutron Transport via PINNs

A complete from-scratch implementation of Physics-Informed Neural Networks (PINNs) to solve neutron diffusion equations — the fundamental PDEs governing neutron behavior in nuclear reactors. Three progressively complex solvers: 1D one-group, 2D one-group, and 1D two-group coupled diffusion. The network learns correct flux distributions purely from the physics equation, with no simulation data required. Validated against exact analytical solutions with L₂ error metrics and publication-quality plots.
